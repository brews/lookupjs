Sacramento & Colorado River>> Paleo ENSO

This version of lookup focuses on patterns between paleo-reconstructions of the Sacramento River ("Sacramento"), the Colorado River ("LeesFerry"), and coastal surface air temperature in the Gulf of Alaska ("GOA").

The data is described in detail below.

"Sacramento" is a tree-ring reconstructed time series of Sacramento River water year natural streamflow (Meko et al. 2001). The data has been transformed into rank-percentiles. See here for further discussion.

"LeesFerry" is a tree-ring reconstructed time series of water year Colorado River natural streamflow at Lee's Ferry (Meko et al. 2007). The data has been transformed into rank-percentiles. See here for further discussion.

"GOA" is tree-ring reconstructed coastal surface temperatures from the Gulf of Alaska (Wilson et al. 2007). The data has been transformed into rank-percentiles.
